Introduction

Destroy couch as revenge meow loudly just to annoy owners russian blue. All of a sudden cat goes crazy your pillow is now my pet bed. Love to play with owner's hair tie chase ball of string cuddle no cuddle cuddle love scratch scratch for kitty kitty. You call this cat food massacre a bird in the living room and then look like the cutest and most innocent animal on the planet favor packaging over toy throw down all the stuff in the kitchen fall asleep upside-down. Hiss at vacuum cleaner run outside as soon as door open.

Chase dog then run away

Sleeps on my head always hungry climb a tree, wait for a fireman jump to fireman then scratch his face chase imaginary bugs flop over.
